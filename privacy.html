TaxLedger

Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 18, 2026

In short: TaxLedger does not collect, transmit, or sell any of your personal or financial data. Everything you enter — transaction amounts, tax profiles, notes, and categories — stays stored locally on your device. We have no servers, no user accounts, and no analytics or advertising SDKs integrated into this app.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

TaxLedger is a local-first calculator and record-keeping tool. We do not operate a backend server for this app, and the app does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal identifying information to use any feature.

We do not collect, and have no access to:

  • Your name, email address, or contact information
  • Business names, addresses, or financial account details
  • Device identifiers, advertising identifiers, or location data
  • Usage analytics, crash reports, or behavioral tracking data

2. Information You Enter Into the App

To use TaxLedger's calculators, you enter transaction amounts and select or create tax rate profiles (state, county, city combinations). When logging a transaction, you may add an optional note and assign a category (Retail, Service, Market/Vendor, or Other).

All of this information is stored exclusively on your device, in the app's local storage (the iOS Documents directory), using standard on-device data files. It is never transmitted to us or to any third party. We have no ability to view, access, or recover this data — if you delete the app, this data is permanently deleted along with it, and we cannot restore it.

3. Exporting Your Data

TaxLedger lets you export your logged transactions as a CSV file or a PDF summary report. When you use this feature, the app generates the file on your device and hands it to Apple's standard system share sheet. Where that exported file goes next — email, Files app, AirDrop, cloud storage, printing, or elsewhere — is entirely your choice, made through Apple's share sheet. TaxLedger itself does not upload, transmit, or otherwise send this file anywhere on your behalf.

4. Reference Data (State, County, and City Tax Rates)

TaxLedger includes a bundled reference dataset of state, county, and city sales tax rates, used only to help you pre-fill an estimated rate when creating a tax profile. This dataset ships inside the app itself and is not fetched from any server, does not involve your personal data in any way, and does not change based on anything you enter.

5. Third-Party Services

TaxLedger does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. We do not use tools such as AdMob, Facebook SDK, Google Analytics, or similar services. The only third-party system involved when you use the export feature is Apple's own system share sheet, governed by Apple's own privacy practices.

6. Children's Privacy

TaxLedger is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any information from children. Because the app does not collect data from any user regardless of age, this is not a practical concern, but we state it here for clarity.

7. Data Deletion

Because all data lives only on your device, you are always in full control of it. To delete all data created by TaxLedger, simply delete the app from your device. Since we do not retain any copy of your data on any server, there is no separate deletion request process — deleting the app is deletion of your data.

8. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes — for example, if a future version of the app adds an optional cloud sync feature — we will update this page and revise the effective date above. Continued use of the app after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
